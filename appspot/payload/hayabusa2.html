A very long journey of Hayabusa 2
Hayabusa 2 is an asteroid explorer developed by the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) as a successor to the asteroid explorer "Hayabusa" (No. 20 scientific satellite MUSES-C).
It was launched on H-IIA Launch Vehicle No. 26 from the Tanegashima Space Center large rocket launch site on 3 December 2014.
Landing and sample return to the Earth's nearby asteroid "1999 JU3" are planned.
